Cardiovascular medications that may increase body weight:
Beta-blockers:
Propranolol may increase body weight
Atenolol may increase body weight
Metoprolol may increase body weight
Carvedilol may not increase body weight
Older and/or less lipophilic dihydropyridine (“dipine”) calcium channel blockers like Nifedipine and Amlodipine may increase body weight due to edema possibly because they are more vasodilatory, compared to nondihydropyridines and lipophilic dihydropyridines.
